Foundation Years Programme @ DNS

At Discovery National School, our Foundation Years Programme is thoughtfully designed to nurture young learners through joyful, meaningful, and hands-on experiences. We believe that the early years lay the foundation for lifelong learning, confidence, and character.

Our curriculum seamlessly integrates Literacy and Numeracy with Montessori-inspired learning experiences, encouraging children to explore, discover, and learn at their own pace. Through Creative Arts, Music, Dance, free play, and Yoga, we foster creativity, self-expression, coordination, physical fitness, and emotional well-being.

Every classroom is a vibrant learning environment where curiosity is celebrated, independence is encouraged, and every child is supported to develop academically, socially, emotionally, and physically. Our experienced educators create engaging experiences that inspire children to become confident, compassionate, and lifelong learners.

Language & Expression

Children develop strong reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills through storytelling, phonics, conversations, rhymes, role play, and interactive classroom experiences that nurture confidence and effective communication.

Discovering Creativity

Creativity flourishes through art, craft, dance, and imaginative play, allowing children to express themselves confidently while developing innovation, collaboration, and self-expression.

Numbers & Reasoning

Children explore numbers through hands-on activities, learning materials, puzzles, games, and practical experiences that develop logical reasoning, problem-solving, and mathematical confidence.

Social & Emotional Learning

Our programme nurtures kindness, empathy, resilience, independence, teamwork, respect, and emotional well-being, helping every child build meaningful relationships and a positive self-image.

Physical Fitness

Through Physical Training (PT), yoga, outdoor play, games, and movement activities, children develop coordination, strength, balance, flexibility, discipline, and lifelong healthy habits.

Environmental Learning

Children discover the wonders of nature, science, culture, and their surroundings through observation, experiments, exploration, and inquiry-based learning, encouraging curiosity and global awareness.

Chairman's Message

"The future belongs to those who are inspired to learn today."

It gives me immense pride and pleasure to welcome you to Discovery National School.

For years, through Discovery Montessori Preschool in KR Puram, we have been committed to nurturing young learners during their most formative years. This journey has strengthened our belief that every child deserves an education that goes beyond textbooks—one that inspires curiosity, builds character, and prepares them for an ever-evolving world.

With this vision, Discovery National School was established in 2024 with a commitment to providing quality education that blends strong academic foundations with creativity, values, life skills, and holistic development. Our goal is to create an environment where children feel safe, respected, encouraged, and motivated to discover their unique potential.

At Discovery National School, we believe that every child learns differently. We therefore strive to foster critical thinking, confidence, collaboration, and a lifelong love for learning through innovative teaching practices and meaningful learning experiences. Alongside academic excellence, we place equal importance on discipline, integrity, empathy, and social responsibility.

Our dedicated team of educators works in partnership with parents to ensure that every child receives the guidance, encouragement, and opportunities needed to flourish academically and personally.

As Chairman, my vision is to build an institution that is recognised not only for its academic standards but also for nurturing compassionate, confident, and future-ready individuals who contribute positively to society.

I warmly invite you to become a part of the Discovery National School family and join us in shaping young minds for a brighter tomorrow.

Warm Regards,

Mr. Ranjit
