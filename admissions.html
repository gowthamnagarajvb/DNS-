FAQ’s

What is the admission procedure?

The admission process begins with booking an online counselling session with an expert followed by collecting the school prospectus and brochure, then submitting the admission form. Finally, enrol your child for the Skill Assessment Session to evaluate their individual needs.

What are the school timings?

Primary:

  • Mon to Fri: 8:15 AM to 3:30 PM
  • Sat: 8:15 AM to 12:30 PM (1st & 3rd Saturdays are OFF)

Pre-Primary:

  • Mon to Fri: 8:15 AM to 12:30 PM
  • Sat: All Saturdays are OFF

What is the age eligibility criteria for admission?

For Nursery, the child should have completed 3 years of age as of June 1st of the academic year. For LKG, the child should have completed 4 years of age as of June 1st. The age criteria for higher grades scales accordingly.

What is the teacher-student ratio at Discovery National School?

We maintain an optimal teacher-student ratio of 1:15. This ensures that every child receives personalized attention, care, and individual guidance tailored to their learning pace.

Which documents are required for seeking admission?

Required documents include the Birth Certificate, Aadhaar Card (Student & Parents), 4 Passport-size photographs, Previous School Report Card, Transfer Certificate, and Vaccination Record.

Are transport facilities available?

Yes. Discovery National School provides safe and reliable transportation for students across selected routes. Our buses are operated by experienced drivers and female attendants to ensure a comfortable and secure journey. For route availability, pick-up points, and transport fees, please contact the school office.
